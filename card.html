white lion

The white lion is a rare color mutation of the lion...

  • Habitat: Timbavati region of South Africa
  • Diet: White lions are carnivorous and are meat eating animals.
  • predator: Humans
  • endangered: less than 300 White Lions worldwide.

No truly white lions were captured until 1975 when a litter containing 2 white cubs was found at Timbavati Game Reserve, adjacent to Kruger National Park. The white cubs were discovered by researcher Chris McBride. Their story is documented in the book "The White Lions of Timbavati
